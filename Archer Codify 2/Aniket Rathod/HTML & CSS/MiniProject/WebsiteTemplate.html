Battle Arena

Time to BRAWL! Team up with your friends and get ready for epic multiplayer MAYHEM! Brawl Stars is the newest game from the makers of Clash of Clans and Clash Royale. Jump into your favorite game mode and play quick matches with your friends. Shoot 'em up, blow 'em up, punch 'em out and win the BRAWL.

Game Modes

Showdown

A battle royale-style game mode involving one, two, or three Brawlers per team. Ten (twelve in Trio Showdown) players battle for survival, with Power Cube boxes scattered across the map providing attack and life boosts.

Know More

Brawl Ball

A soccer game mode where two teams of three Brawlers score points by shooting a ball into the opponents' goal. The game ends if one team scores two points, or if one team has a point advantage after two minutes. A variation with five Brawlers, as well as Basketball and volleyball varieties, were added later.

Know More

Gem Grab

Gems spawn in the middle of the map, and two teams of three Brawlers must collect the highest number of Gems possible. A team wins if it accumulates more than ten Gems for 15 seconds. A variation with five Brawlers was added later.

Know More

Wipeout and Bounty

Deathmatch game modes. In Wipeout, two teams of either three or five must defeat 10 enemy Brawlers. In Bounty, teams must earn 20 points by defeating Brawlers, who become worth more points for each kill.

Know More

Heist

Two teams of three Brawlers have must destroy the opponents' highly durable safe located near their spawn point, before the enemy team can destroy their own safe.

Know More

Knockout

A deathmatch game mode where players cannot respawn and rounds are won by defeating the entire enemy team. The first team to win two rounds wins the game. A variation with five Brawlers is unique in that each elimination causes the remaining teammates to gain extra damage and health.
