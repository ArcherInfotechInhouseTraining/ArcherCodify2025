Our Services

As a Full Stack Java Developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, I offer a range of services to help bring your digital ideas to life. Here’s what I can do for you:

1. Full Stack Web Development

Building dynamic and responsive web applications using Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like Spring Boot and Hibernate.. End-to-end solutions for front-end and back-end development, ensuring a seamless user experience.

2. Android App Development

Creating intuitive and high-performance mobile applications for Android devices. Custom app development to meet business needs, including UI/UX design and database integration. As per requirement needed

3. Custom Web Solutions

Developing tailored web applications that solve specific problems for businesses or individuals. From e-commerce platforms to content management systems, I can help turn your unique ideas into fully functional applications.

4. API Development & Integration

Building RESTful APIs for efficient communication between the front-end and back-end systems. Integrating third-party APIs into your application to extend functionality as per your requirements.

5. Database Management

Designing and optimizing databases to ensure data is stored securely and accessed efficiently. Working with relational databases such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, and NoSQL databases when required.

6. UI/UX Design

Crafting clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces to ensure your web and mobile applications are visually appealing and easy to navigate. Focusing on responsive design to ensure your application works seamlessly across all devices.
