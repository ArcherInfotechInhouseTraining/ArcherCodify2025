About Me

Hello! I'm Devika Prashant Naik, and I've been weaving stories through the elegant art of Kathak since childhood. Dance has been my companion and teacher, imparting grace, discipline, and a deep appreciation for cultural heritage.

In parallel to my journey as a dancer, I discovered a profound passion for the world of coding and programming. Just as dance is an expressive language of the body, coding allows me to create, innovate, and solve problems in the digital realm.

By fusing these two seemingly different worlds, I bring a unique perspective to both my dance and tech projects. Whether it's performing intricate footwork on stage or developing seamless code, I strive for excellence, creativity, and a touch of artistry in everything I do.

Welcome to my portfolio, where tradition meets technology and passion knows no bounds.
