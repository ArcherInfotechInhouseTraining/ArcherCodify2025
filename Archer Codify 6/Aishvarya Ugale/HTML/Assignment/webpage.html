Welcome to the DYPCOEI Campus! Dr. D. Y. Patil Educational Federation’s Dr. D. Y. Patil College of Engineering and Innovation, Talegaon, Pune, was established in 2014. Spread over 3.5 acres, the college offers four Engineering disciplines and is affiliated with the Savitribai Phule Pune University. This institute is approved by AICTE and recognized by the Government of Maharashtra.

With excellent infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and a team of motivated and dedicated faculty members, the college ensures holistic development. Students are actively encouraged to participate in national and state-level co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
