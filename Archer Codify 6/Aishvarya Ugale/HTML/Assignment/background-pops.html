Dr. D. Y. Patil Educational Federation’s, Dr. D. Y. Patil College of Engineering and Innovation, Varale, Talegaon, Pune was established in 2014. The college spreads over 3.5 acres of land with four Engineering disciplines. This Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and is affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University. The college has excellent & ambient infrastructure with well-equipped laboratories, well-qualified, motivated, and dedicated faculty members serving in the Institute. Students are encouraged to actively participate in National and State level co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
